Valparai

Valparai, a land where clouds descend, Where tea gardens stretch without an end. Elephants stroll through misty trails, And cool winds hum forgotten tales. A paradise where nature quietly prevails.

Athirappalli

From Valparai’s hills where tea leaves sway, To Athirappalli’s falls in a silver spray. Misty roads and forest calls entwine, Where clouds kiss trees and rivers shine. Nature’s magic flows in every line.
